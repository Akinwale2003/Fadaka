Why it works

FADAKA builds on women's demonstrated saving tendencies and social capital — combining financial tools with community trust to create a powerful safety net.

  • Women save more consistently in goal-focused structures.
  • Community donations are more likely when stories are verified and visible.
  • Mobile-first design removes barriers to access.
